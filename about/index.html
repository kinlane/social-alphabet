Social Alphabet

I am always looking for ways to help folks understand the exploitation that is going on rigt beneath their fingertips. Recently, I cut out the logos for some of the top tech platforms, and turned their letters into my own alphabet.

Social ALphabet was born, and is giving me a new way to help demonstrate the potential of these platforms we depend on each day, and we let into our personal and professional lives, giving them unprecendent access to our lives.

Maybe you can make your own images and share with the world. I will work ona better sharing mechanism, but for now you can just take a screen shot and share via your favorite exploitative platform with all your friends and family.
